About Which Sandwich

Which Sandwich is a website for designing, sharing and rating sandwiches. Users can list create sandwiches using an expandable list of ingredients. A picture of their sandwich would be uploaded. Users can vote on other's sandwiches usling a like system, as well as add sandwiches to their favourites. Visitors can see a ranking of the top, latest and most controversial sandwiches. The goal is to help people keep in touch with the latest sandwich trends, while developing their creativity. It can be a valuable tool for small business owners to gauge their market. It could also come in handy to please your family and friends with awesome custom made sandwiches.
